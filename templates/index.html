FWI Prediction for Algerian Forest Fires Dataset

Data Set Information

The dataset includes 244 instances from two regions in Algeria: Bejaia (northeast) and Sidi Bel-abbes (northwest), with 122 instances per region. The data covers the period from June 2012 to September 2012 and consists of 11 attributes and 1 output class.

The instances are classified into two categories: Fire (138 cases) and Not Fire (106 cases).
